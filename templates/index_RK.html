What am I looking at?

This site is an interactive dashboard to allow anybody interested in Chicago Crime to be able to click around and easily get information. The data diaplyed reflects reported incidents of crime that occured in the City of Chicago in 2020.

Raw data for this website was supplied by https://data.cityofchicago.org.

Why is this useful?

As residents of Chicago, we found it fitting to create an interactive dashboard to visualize Crime Data in our city. We wanted to provide a place for anybody to be able to click around and easily get information if concerned about crime rates in Chicago.

If you are a new resident or are looking to potentially move to Chicago, we highly reccomend utilizing this dashboard to get a better understanding of crime rates around various neighborhoods in Chicago.

How do I use this dashboard?

Navigate to the Filter Search on the left. Here you will notice various search bars. These are filters you can use to create unique visualizations of Chicago Crime data. The filters you place will dictate how the graphs, charts, and map below look.

Try it on your own! Use the filters o drill down into the data. Here are some helpful questions to get you started:

1. Which month are you most interested in seeing data for?

2. What neighborhood do you live in?

3. When was the last fire in downtown Chicago?

4. Which neighborhood is the safest? Which has the least ammount of crimes?

Click around and watch the data come to life in the charts and visualizations!
